Study Hkupop published 2016 Pre-election LegCo survey data-sets

A personal and informal study on the dataset provided in hkupopy web site containing the Legco rolling survey 2016 datasets

Welcome this GitHub Pages.

This web page is just a new hobby to try to analysis the data published by hkupop on the 2016 rolling survey of the public's view on the upcoming Legco. You can get those data in SPSS format or csvy here. In fact it is lonely if I am the only guy doing it.

Current Status

The first datasets was published at 3 p.m. on 15 July 2016. I would try to understand the datasets and reproduce the findings here first.
